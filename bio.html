Biography of William H. “Bill” Louden

Bill Louden is a 40-year veteran of the Digital Media and Internet industries currently serving on the board of Online Blockchain, plc, a UK company.

He is a serial entrepreneur and has served as President of a global business unit for General Electric Company, and senior vice president of a News Corp. Internet division.

Bill is a graduate of Ohio State University with a BA in English and Japanese and holds a Master, International Management degree from the University of Maryland. He recently retired as Department Chair, International Business for Austin Community College and is an adjunct professor in Digital Media Management at St. Edward’s University.

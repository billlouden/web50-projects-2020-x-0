Woodworking

Building heirlooms for my family and yours.

My company, Louden Woodworks, can build custom furniture or crafts to published plans or to your specifications. I prefer to use locally harvested wood from the Central Texas area, especially Pecan, American Elm, or Oak as available. I also work with most hardwoods such as Alder, Poplar, Cherry, Mahogany, or Walnut. More exotic species that I have used for smaller projects include Padauk, PurpleHeart, and Bubinga.

Prices will vary depending on project, design, and wood selection.
